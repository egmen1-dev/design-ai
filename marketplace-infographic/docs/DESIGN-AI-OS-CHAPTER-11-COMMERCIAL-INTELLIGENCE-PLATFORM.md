# DESIGN AI OS — Chapter 11: Commercial Intelligence Platform

## Purpose

Chapter 11 defines the **Commercial Intelligence Platform** — a unified commercial thinking layer in the Design AI Operating System. It does not generate images; it produces commercial intent, specifications, forecasts, and constitution validation for downstream platforms.
