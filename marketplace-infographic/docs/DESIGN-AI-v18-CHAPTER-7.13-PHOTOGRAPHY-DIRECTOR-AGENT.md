# DESIGN AI v18 — Chapter 7.13: Photography Director Agent

## Purpose

Photography Director designs professional commercial product photography. After Story, Scene, and Composition are defined, this agent answers: **"How should this be photographed to look like professional commercial photography?"**

## Mission

Design photographic style that builds trust, looks realistic, emphasizes quality, supports product benefits, and matches marketplace buyer expectations — never like AI-generated art.

## Module

Implemented as `photography-director-agent-*`, extending Ch 4.13 `commercial-photo-director-*` and Ch 6.9 `photography-planning-stage-*`.

The buyer must believe the product truly exists — not that the image was generated by AI. Photography Director makes prior work look like a professional commercial photo session.
